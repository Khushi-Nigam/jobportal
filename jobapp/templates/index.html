Why an ATS-Friendly Resume is Important?

Only 2% of resumes make it past the first round. Be in the top 2%.

Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to filter resumes. If your resume isn't ATS-optimized, it might never reach a recruiter. Here's why an ATS-friendly resume matters:

  • Keyword Matching: ATS scans for job-related keywords, so including them increases your chances of being noticed.
  • Simple Formatting: Complex designs can confuse ATS. A clean, structured resume ensures it’s read correctly.
  • More Interviews: An ATS-friendly resume boosts your chances of being shortlisted for interviews.
